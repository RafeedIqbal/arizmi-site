# Arizmi

> Your technical co-founder, combining both business strategy and software engineering to turn your idea into a successful business.

## Services

- Full-stack app and website development for start-ups

- Mobile and web application engineering
- Post-launch support and growth partnership

2. **Plan & Build** — We shape the product strategy and build the right solution.
3. **Launch & Grow** — We launch with you and support the next stage.
